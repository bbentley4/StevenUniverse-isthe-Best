Steven Universe and the Crystal Gems

Steven is a young half-human, half-gem growing up in Beach City, Delmarva. He's raised by three extraterrestrial beings who call themselves the Crystal Gems. These Crystal Gems have tasked themselves with protecting humanity from monsters... and other threats. This show has beautiful representation of CPTSD, trauma, LGBT love and heartbreak, family conflicts, and what it means to be human.
